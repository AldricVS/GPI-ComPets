Tuto Compet's

The purpose of this simulation is to train an animal.The animal can interact with the red, orange and green boxes. The gray squares are simple squares where the animal can only move, the black squares are walls.A red square is a square where the animal can do a bad action, a green square is a square where the animal can do a good action and an orange square is where it can do a good or bad action.


You can either reward the animal or punish it with buttons.


You can observe the evolution of your pet's learning with the obedience gauge, and with the help of the graph of the pet's last action history. But pay attention to the pet's well being gauge.

Obedience gauge:

  • Decrease when:
    • the animal is punished when it does nothing
    • the animal is punished when it does a good deed
    • the animal does a bad deed and is not punished
  • Increment when:
    • the animal is rewarded for a good deed
    • the animal does a bad deed and is punished
    • the animal does a good deed but is not rewarded

Well-being gauge:

  • Decrease when:
    • the animal is punished when it does nothing
    • the animal is punished while doing a good deed
    • the animal does good deeds but is not rewarded
  • Increment when:
    • the animal is rewarded for a good deed
    • the animal does nothing
